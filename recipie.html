My yummy pizza recipe

Heres What you'll need:

  • 140 grams of pizza dough
  • 60 grams of pepperoni
  • 60 grams san marzano tomatos (in sauce form)
  • 60G mozzarella cheese
  • 40g pecorino ramano cheese
  • salt, papper and italian seasoning
  • lastly, loooove

How to put it all together:

  1. Play with the dough and get it into a nice circle
  2. Ladel the 60g scoop of tomato sauce onto the pizza
  3. Use the ladel to evenly spread the sauce, we're looking for 100% coverage here folks
  4. Then grab the mozz and sprinkle that sucka all over
  5. Now, take the fine pecorino ramano and cover all the gaps, if you see sauce it needs more cheese
  6. Put the pepperonis on and dust some of the salt, pepper and italian on dat thang
  7. Now you're ready to pop it into the oven at the highest temp for like 10-12 mins
  8. You wanna see golden crust and a nice bubbly crust
  9. Cool it down for 10 to 15 mins once done, and then enjoy!
  10. Serve with:
    • Buffalo wings
    • Beer
